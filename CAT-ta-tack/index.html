FARTO MAN

  • Originality: In this game, you get to protect the main character through the use of drag and using your pointer.
  • Technical Merit: The development of this game is different and more complex than the previous ones. It contains multiple game states (6 to be exact). Each state inter connected and communicated with each other. The game play relies heavily on the use of the mouse and the movement of the cursor. While you can freely move the cursor around, the main sprite itself will not follow you, so the only way to get the sprite to move around is to use the drag and drop feature. Due to the fact that you are constantly clicking on the main sprite and moving the cursor, the enemy sprite will then follow the cursor and the main sprite you are playing as.
  • Prototype Postmortem: I learned how to handle events related to the cursor on different types of game objects. I also learned how to connect multiple states and call each one from a different state. I also learned how to efficiently use time events.

  • Prototype Goal: The game mechanic is moving the cursor around the screen in random patterns to avoid the enemy sprite.
  • Player Experience Goals: The player should experience thrill, pressure, and physical dexterity.
  • Gameplay: The actions the player can make is moving the mouse or cursor around the screen in random motions. The player must click on the main sprite and move it in random patterns to avoid the crazy cat lady. The player uses either the mouse or the track pad and that is it! Strategies: The player could attempt to dodge the enemy sprite by constantly moving the cursor in random motions in order to avoid getting caught.
  • Strategies: There are no strategies in this game.
  • Story/Setting/Premise: The world is a beautiful place, especially when there are cats in it. One day, a lady who is known to have psychological issues escaped from an asylum. She happened to have a huge obsession with cats and would not rest until she captured all the cats in the world and put them under her command. She eventually captured all the cats. OR SO SHE THOUGHT! One day, as she was walking on the streets with her army of cats, she noticed a cat that was free from her control and she could not accept that! So she began a quest to hunt down the last free cat in the world! The fate of the last cat in the world lies in the hands of the player!
